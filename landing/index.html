Built for people, not project managers.

Most planning apps were designed for teams with dedicated PMs and hours to spare. DateWheel was built for the rest of us — the freelancer juggling three clients, the small team trying to ship something, the person who just needs to know if they're on track.

🎡

The wheel makes sense instantly

Add your tasks and they fill the circle. A bigger slice means more time. It's the kind of thing you understand in two seconds, not two weeks of training.

📅

Three ways to look at your schedule

Wheel, calendar, Gantt chart. Switch between them with one tap. Same project, different lens — pick whichever one helps you right now.

🔒

Move one date, everything else follows

Lock the timeline and slide any task — the rest shift automatically. No more manually nudging every single date because the kickoff moved by a week.

🔔

Reminders that actually remind you

Set a heads-up on any task — 3 days before, a week before, whatever you need. The app pings you so you don't have to keep the date in your head.

◆

Milestones for the moments that matter

Drop a milestone on any date — a launch, a review, a deadline. It shows up on the wheel and across every view so you never lose sight of what the whole thing is building toward.

📤

Share it however you want

Export to PDF, Excel, or a calendar file your whole team can open. It plays nicely with everything else you're already using.
