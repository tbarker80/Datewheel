Smart Scheduling

Business rules
built right in

DateWheel knows about weekends, holidays, and task dependencies — so your deadlines stay realistic.

  • 📅

    No Weekend End Dates

    Toggle this on and any task end date that falls on a Saturday or Sunday automatically shifts to the following Monday. The original intended duration is preserved so rescheduling from the start date still works correctly.

  • 🗓️

    Holiday Exclusion Pro

    Choose your country's public holiday calendar — US, Canada, Mexico, or China — and DateWheel excludes those days from business-day duration calculations automatically.

  • ⚡

    Lead / Lag (Task Overlap & Gap)

    Set a negative lag (lead) so the next task starts before the current one ends — great for parallel work. Or add positive lag so the next task begins N days after its predecessor finishes.

  • 🔗

    Cascade Scheduling

    Extend a task end date and every downstream task shifts forward in lock-step. Shrink a task and the gap closes automatically — no manual date-dragging across the chain.

Touch Gestures

Designed for
natural touch

Every interaction in DateWheel is a direct gesture — no menus, no forms, just drag and done.

👆

Tap

Select a task to highlight it and show its duration. Tap again to deselect.

↔️

Drag

Drag the blue dot (start), orange dot (end), or boundary dots to reshape any task in real time.

🔒

Drag (Locked)

With timeline locked, drag anywhere on the wheel ring to slide the entire project forward or backward.

🤏

Pinch

Pinch to zoom in on the calendar view (0.65×–2.2×) or the Gantt chart (1×–8×) for day-level detail.

✋

Pan

When zoomed into the calendar or Gantt, pan with one finger to navigate. Bounds-clamped so you never scroll off the content.

🗜️

Long Press

Long-press a saved project or template to delete it — with a confirmation prompt before anything is removed.
